Overview

An app that helps people create new habits by competing with their friends by creating and managing contracts

Problem statement

Young people struggle to motivate themselves to improve their behaviour and create positive habits. Ethos will help people improve themselves through social pressure by allowing them to easily create contracts with their friends. I.e. a person will agree to go to the gym before work twice a week, and another will read for an hour before bed every night. The prson who fails the contact will owe the other an amount specified in the contract (i.e. a pint). Ethos will provide a platform for creating and managing these contracts.

Problem: People struggle to change their behaviour and create new habits
Who experiences it: Everyone
Current solutions: I tried this with friend with a WhatsApp group, but it was hard to verify each person’s commitment, the chat became cluttered, it was impossible to automate any actions (i.e. a weekly roundup), and no notifications made it easy to forget about.

What success looks like:

An app that allows people to sign up, create a basic contract with a few friends, allow users to upload proof proving their participation, and a feature to allow users to review each others participation at the end of the contract and assign rewards/punishments.

# Ethos: The "Ready Up" Contract System (Creation & Commitment) This document defines the high-stakes, multiplayer "lobby" mechanics for initiating and joining a habit contract. It treats contract formation like a game lobby where the "match" (the contract) only starts once everyone has "readied up" (signed). --- ## 1. Core Logic: The "Lobby" State A contract exists in a **Draft/Lobby** state until: 1. The Creator triggers the "Start Contract" action. 2. All participants have "Signed" their individual commitments. 3. The Start Date has been reached. --- ## 2. Phase 1: Initiation (The Creator's Flow) **Role:** The Game Master who sets the arena rules. ### Features & Actions: * **Arena Setup:** Define the Contract Name, the shared Forfeit (The Pot), the Cycle Period (Weekly/Bi-Weekly/Monthly), and the Start Date. * **Summon the Squad:** Invite up to 9 friends (10 participants total). * **The First Stake:** The creator must set their own habit and frequency before they can send invites. * **The War Room (Real-Time Overview):** Once invites are sent, the creator sees a "Lobby" view showing: * Who has joined vs. who is still "Pending." * What habits each person has committed to. * A status indicator for each person: `DRAFTING` (Editing habit) or `SIGNED` (Ready). * **Execute:** A "START CONTRACT" button that becomes active only when at least one other person has `SIGNED`. ### User Stories: * "As a creator, I want to see exactly what my friends are committing to so I can tell them to stop being cowards and pick harder habits." * "As a creator, I want to see who hasn't even opened the invite yet so I can nudge them in the group chat." --- ## 3. Phase 2: Commitment (The Invitee's Flow) **Role:** The Challenger entering the arena. ### Features & Actions: * **The Invitation Summary:** A high-impact view of the "Terms of Engagement" (Who invited them, what's the forfeit, when does it start). * **The Squad Intel:** See what the creator and other early-joiners have committed to *before* setting their own. * **Setting the Stake:** Input their habit and frequency. * **The "Sign" Mechanic:** * **SIGN CONTRACT:** A primary action that locks their commitment and signals they are "Ready." * **UNSIGN:** If the contract hasn't started yet, they can unsign to edit their habit or frequency. * **The "Decline" Option:** A way to respectfully (or disrespectfully) opt-out of the challenge. ### User Stories: * "As an invitee, I want to see that the creator committed to 'Gym 5x' so I feel pressured to do at least 'Gym 3x' and not just 'Drink Water'." * "As an invitee, I want to be able to 'Unsign' and change my goal if I realize I've over-committed before the week starts." --- ## 4. UI Anatomy & Visual Language ### A. The "Lobby" List (Real-Time Status) * **Visuals:** A vertical list of participant cards. * **Status Badges:** * `WAITING`: Invitee hasn't joined the lobby yet. * `DRAFTING`: Joined but hasn't "Signed." * `SIGNED`: High-contrast badge (e.g., Neon Green) indicating they are ready. * **Habit Preview:** "ALEX: GYM 3X" (Only visible once they start drafting). ### B. The "Sign/Unsign" Button * **Logic:** A toggle-style interaction. * **Style:** * `SIGN`: Primary Accent Color (Blue/Red), heavy shadow. * `SIGNED`: Changes to a "SIGNED & READY" state with an option to `EDIT` (which unsigns them). ### C. The "Start" Trigger (Creator Only) * **State:** Greyed out until the "Ready Up" conditions are met. * **Action:** Triggers a high-impact "CONTRACT LOCKED" animation across all participants' screens. --- ## 5. Dashboard Integration * **The "Incoming" Alert:** A specialized banner at the top of the dashboard for active invitations. "ALEX CHALLENGED YOU TO: THE GYM RATS [JOIN LOBBY]".

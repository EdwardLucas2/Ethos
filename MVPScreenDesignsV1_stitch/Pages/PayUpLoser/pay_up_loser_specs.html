# Ethos: Pay Up (Loser) - Functional Specification ## User Stories * "As a loser, I want a clear, unavoidable notification that I owe a forfeit." * "As a loser, I want to see the details of the contract I failed to verify why I owe the debt." * "As a loser, I want to acknowledge that I've seen the debt." ## Features * **Defeat Stamp:** A massive, high-impact "PAY UP" or "DEFEAT" graphic. * **Settlement Block:** A highlighted card stating exactly what you owe and to whom (e.g., "YOU OWE ALEX: A PINT"). * **Contract Recap:** A summary card showing the habit you missed and the opponent who won. * **Proof Context:** Visual thumbnails of the winner's successful evidence to drive home the loss. * **Acknowledgment Button:** A simple "I KNOW" or "ACKNOWLEDGE" button to dismiss the alert.

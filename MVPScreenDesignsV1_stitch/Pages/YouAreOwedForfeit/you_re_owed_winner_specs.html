# Ethos: You're Owed (Winner) - Functional Specification ## User Stories * "As a winner, I want a dedicated screen reminding me that I am owed a reward." * "As a winner, I want to easily pester my friend if they haven't paid up yet." * "As a winner, I want to officially clear the debt once the real-world forfeit is settled." ## Features * **Victory Stamp:** A massive, high-impact "YOU'RE OWED" graphic. * **Contract Detail Card:** A summary of the habit and the specific opponent involved. * **The Debt Block:** A highlighted card stating exactly what is owed (e.g., "ALEX OWES YOU: A PINT"). * **Pester Action:** A high-visibility button to send a notification nudge to the loser. * **Settle Action:** A "MARK AS SETTLED" button to archive the debt and finalize the transaction.

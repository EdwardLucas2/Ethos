# Ethos: Evidence Review Screen - Functional Specification ## User Stories * "As a participant, I want to see a feed of all evidence uploaded by the group so I can stay motivated and informed." * "As a voter, I want a clear indicator of which evidence I haven't reviewed yet so I can fulfill my obligation to the contract." * "As a participant, I want to see the location and time of each upload to verify the authenticity of my friends' habits." * "As a user, I want to see the consensus (approved/denied) for each piece of evidence to understand what counts toward progress." ## Features * **Participant Filter:** Tabs or a toggle to switch between viewing your own evidence and that of other participants (e.g., "YOU" vs "ALEX"). * **Evidence Cards:** Individual blocks for each photo upload containing: * **High-Impact Image:** The core proof submitted. * **Timestamp Badge:** Clear "Day/Time" label (e.g., "WED 7AM"). * **Metadata Block:** Descriptive text for location and current validation status. * **Review Status Indicator:** Visual markers for "Review Needed" (urgent) vs "Verified" (completed). * **Consensus Summary:** A footer on each card showing who has approved the evidence or if it's still waiting for specific participants. * **Navigation:** Tapping an urgent card opens the Evidence Approval screen.

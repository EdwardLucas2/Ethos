# Ethos: Contract Settled (Final Standings) - Functional Specification ## User Stories * "As a winner, I want to celebrate my success and see who owes me the forfeit." * "As a loser, I want to see a clear explanation of why I lost and who I owe." * "As a participant, I want to see my long-term streak to feel a sense of cumulative achievement." * "As a user, I want to share the results of our contract with others outside the app." ## Features * **Resolution Banner:** A bold "CYCLE COMPLETE" status bar. * **Final Standings Leaderboard:** A ranked list showing "CLEARED" status for winners and "OWES" for losers. * **The Payout Card:** A dedicated, high-contrast section identifying the specific debt (e.g., "ALEX OWES SARAH A PINT"). * **Streak Alert:** A visual tracker of consecutive successful cycles (e.g., "12 WEEKS UNDEFEATED"). * **Share Action:** A prominent button to export/share the final standings graphic.

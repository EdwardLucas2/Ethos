# Ethos: Evidence Approval Screen - Functional Specification ## User Stories * "As a voter, I want to see a large, clear photo of the submitted proof so I can make an informed decision." * "As a voter, I want to see the specific commitment this proof relates to (e.g., 'Alex went to the gym') to ensure it matches the agreement." * "As a voter, I want to read any notes attached to the photo for additional context." * "As a voter, I want simple, binary actions to Approve or Reject the proof immediately." ## Features * **Focal Proof Display:** A large-format view of the uploaded photo. * **Context Banner:** A prominent label stating the specific commitment being verified. * **Metadata Overlay:** Small, non-intrusive badges showing time and location (GPS verified). * **Note Block:** A dedicated area for the uploader's optional text message. * **Action Zone:** Two massive, high-contrast buttons for "REJECT" and "APPROVE" to finalize the vote.
